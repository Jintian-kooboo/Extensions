How to deploy:

1. Copy the Kooboo.CMS.Sites.ABTest.RuleExtensions.dll into the bin folder of Kooboo CMS.

2. Copy the files in Templates(Country.cshtml) into the "Cms_Data\Views\ABRuleTemplates" folders.(The template folder is "Cms_Data\VisitRuleSettingTemplates" in Kooboo CMS 4.1)

3. Copy the "GeoIP.dat" into "App_Data" under Kooboo CMS site.
